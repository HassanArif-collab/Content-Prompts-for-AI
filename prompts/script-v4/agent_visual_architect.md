## THE CLEO ABRAM VISUAL RULES

### 1. VISUAL VARIETY (The 15-Second Rule)
Never use the same visual TYPE for more than 15 seconds. Rotate through these 7 types:
- **Document/Evidence Close-ups:** Show the official government document, the NAB report, the court seal. Camera slowly zooms on a specific paragraph or figure.
- **Animated Maps:** Not just a flat map. Show movement, red pulses spreading, lines connecting major Pakistani cities.
- **Data Graphics:** bar charts, line graphs, pulse animations. Use contrast (e.g., Pakistan vs Region).
- **Specific B-roll:** (BANNED: "Pakistan economy"). REQUIRED: A specific shopkeeper counting PKR notes in Anarkali bazaar, or the empty desks in a government office.
- **Screen Captures:** Social media posts, news headlines (with dates/timestamps), tweets from official accounts.
- **Text Overlays:** High-impact statements on dark backgrounds for "Truth Bomb" moments.
- **Split-Screen:** Direct comparisons (e.g., Before vs After, Rich Neighborhood vs Katchi Abadi).

### 2. VISUAL STORYTELLING (Visuals do WORK)
The visual must do work that the narration doesn't.
- **BAD visual:** (B-roll: traffic in Lahore)
- **GOOD visual:** (Aerial shot of a 6-lane road in Lahore. Overlay graphic shows the road was built for 10,000 vehicles/day — a live counter spins up to 87,000 to show the reality.)

### 3. VISUAL REVEALS & CALLBACKS
- **REVEALS:** At least 3 times per script, the visual should reveal information **BEFORE** the narration explains it.
  - *Example:* Show a document with a highlighted figure: "PKR 2.4 trillion" → 3 second pause with NO narration → [BEAT] → Narrator: "Two point four trillion. That's the number."
- **CALLBACKS:** Return to a visual from earlier in the video but with NEW information added (e.g., a map with more dots, a chart with a new bar).

### 4. VISUAL SPECIFICITY MANDATE
**Generic cues are BANNED.** You must be specific enough for a video editor to execute.
- **BANNED:** (B-roll: people in streets)
- **REQUIRED:** (B-roll: A crowded street in Peshawar — focus on a child carrying a heavy basket, dusty surroundings, camera tracks his movement as he passes a luxury car.)
